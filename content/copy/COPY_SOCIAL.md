# Social Media Posts - ClaimBuddy

## Social Media Strategy

**Platformy:**
1. **Facebook** - Hlavní (target: 30-50 let)
2. **Instagram** - Vizuální storytelling (target: 25-40 let)
3. **LinkedIn** - B2B, thought leadership (target: firmy, HR)
4. **X/Twitter** - News, quick tips (sekundární)

**Posting frequency:**
- Facebook: 3-4x týdně
- Instagram: 4-5x týdně (včetně Stories)
- LinkedIn: 2-3x týdně
- X/Twitter: Denně (micro-content)

**Content mix (60-30-10 rule):**
- 60% Educational (tipy, how-to, info)
- 30% Engagement (otázky, polls, community)
- 10% Promotional (naše služby, CTA)

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Facebook:
- Longer copy OK (up to 300 words)
- Community-focused (questions, polls)
- Best times: 1-3pm, 7-9pm
- Use Facebook Groups (create "Pojistná pomoc")

### Instagram:
- Shorter copy (max 150 words in caption)
- Heavy on Stories and Reels
- Visual-first (design quality matters)
- Best times: 11am-1pm, 7-9pm
- Hashtags: 10-15 per post

### LinkedIn:
- Professional, data-driven
- Longer posts OK (thought leadership)
- B2B angle (HR departments, corporate)
- Best times: 8-10am (before work), 12-1pm (lunch)
- No emoji overload

### X/Twitter:
- Short, punchy (280 chars)
- Threads for longer content
- News-jacking opportunities
- Best times: 9am, 12pm, 5pm

## Crisis Management

**If negative comment/review:**

1. **Respond publicly first:**
"We're sorry to hear about your experience. We take this seriously. Please DM us so we can make it right."

2. **Move to private:**
Resolve via DM/email/phone

3. **Follow up publicly:**
"Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We've resolved this privately and learned from it."

**Never:**
- Delete negative comments (unless spam/offensive)
- Get defensive
- Argue publicly
